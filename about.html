About Fintax Support Limited

In 2001, my journey in tax preparation began after my successful completion of the tax preparation course at H&R Block. I was then hired by them as a tax preparer. During my tenure with H&R Block, I've completed numerous courses to increase my knowledge of the tax codes and regulations both at the Federal & State level.

With 24 years experience in tax preparation, I've decided to start a new and exciting chapter by launching Fintax Support Limited, to use the knowledge I've acquired over the years to bring ease and peace of mind to my clients during the tax preparation process.
